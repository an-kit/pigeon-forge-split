# 🎬 THE TRIP THAT COST LOVE AND MONEY
### A Disney-Style Animated Short Film Script
**Written for children ages 6–11**  
**Runtime: ~4 minutes**  
**Format: 2.5D Premium Animation**

---

## 🎨 PRODUCTION BIBLE

| Element | Specification |
|---|---|
| **Style** | Walt Disney Animation meets Pixar Short — soft painterly backgrounds, expressive squash-and-stretch characters, warm cinematic lighting |
| **Emotional Target** | Wonder → Curiosity → Understanding → Gratitude |
| **Story Engine** | A child discovers that every magical memory has an invisible price tag — and that price tag is proof of love |
| **Core Message** | Money is not just numbers. It is time, sacrifice, planning, and love made visible. |
| **Never** | Preachy, scary, dry, lecture-y, or sad |
| **Always** | Visual storytelling first, dialogue second. If you can SHOW it, never SAY it. |

## 🎭 CHARACTER BIBLE

### NIRMAAN (Hero, age 8)
- **Design:** Warm brown skin, perpetually messy hair with one stubborn cowlick, bright red high-top sneakers that squeak when he walks, oversized curiosity-green backpack
- **Personality:** The kid who asks "but WHY though?" about everything. Pure wonder engine. Eyes that go wide like satellite dishes when he learns something new.
- **Animation signature:** Leans forward on his toes when excited. Taps chin when thinking. Does a full-body jump when he understands something.
- **Voice:** Bright, fast-talking, slightly out of breath like he ran here to tell you something

### ANKIT (Dad, mid-30s — Narrator & Guide)
- **Design:** Warm brown skin, kind crinkly eyes, slight stubble, always in a cozy navy hoodie. Has a "I know something wonderful" smile he can't quite contain.
- **Personality:** The dad who makes learning feel like discovering buried treasure. Never talks DOWN to kids — talks WITH them.
- **Animation signature:** Hands always moving when he talks. Puts one hand on Nirmaan's shoulder for important moments. Eyes close briefly when he laughs.
- **Voice:** Warm campfire storyteller energy. Unhurried. Like Morgan Freeman if he coached little league.

### SUHANI (Mom)
- **Design:** Warm brown skin, long dark hair in a loose bun with one strand always escaping, mustard yellow kurta, paint-stained fingertips (she's an artist)
- **Personality:** The quiet emotional anchor of the family. Her hugs stop time.
- **Animation signature:** Always slightly in motion — stirring something, arranging flowers, glancing over with a knowing smile

### TUHI (Age 2, scene stealer)
- **Design:** Chubby cheeks that take up 40% of her face, two lopsided pigtails, permanently wide eyes, clutches a worn stuffed elephant named "Mottu"
- **Personality:** Does not understand money. Does not need to. Radiates pure joy.
- **Animation signature:** Toddles everywhere at full speed. Falls down. Gets up. Keeps going. Claps at random things.

### THE UNCLES & AUNTIES (Supporting ensemble)
- **UNCLE ASHISH:** Tall, booming laugh, checkered shirt, always the first to suggest seconds at dinner
- **UNCLE MONTU:** Round glasses, warm smile, talks with his hands even more than Ankit
- **UNCLE SAUNAK:** Baseball cap turned backwards, most likely to race the kids to the water slide
- **UNCLE AMRISH:** Cardigan, quiet strength, the one who remembers everyone's food allergies
